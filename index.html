About Me

Hi, I'm Bryan Paul, an 18-year-old wildlife photographer dedicated to capturing the raw, quiet moments of the natural world. What started as a hobby has evolved into a journey of patience, observation, and respect for the wild. Through Nocturne Lens, I aim to share the beauty I encounter, hoping to inspire a deeper connection with the animals and landscapes that share our planet.
